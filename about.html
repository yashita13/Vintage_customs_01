WHAT IS VINTAGE CUSTOMS?

Vintage Customs is a captivating garage shop that exudes an air of nostalgia and automotive passion. Established in 2010, this labor of love was founded by myself and my father. Specializing in the restoration of classic and vintage cars, our workshop has become a sanctuary for automotive enthusiasts and collectors alike. With a meticulous attention to detail, we breathe new life into aging beauties, reviving their former glory and preserving their timeless charm. Beyond restoration, we take pride in infusing modern twists into these vintage vehicles, blending the best of old and new to create truly unique automotive works of art. At Vintage Customs, we also serve as a haven for vintage car enthusiasts, offering a carefully curated selection of classic cars for sale and providing a platform for fellow enthusiasts to indulge in their passion for timeless automobiles. Step into our shop, and you'll be transported back in time to an era where craftsmanship and elegance reigned supreme.
